‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import audio files?

To import audio files into Multitrack Player:

  1. Tap the import button in the app
  2. Select audio files from your device's Files app
  3. The files will be imported and added to your project

What audio formats are supported?

Multitrack Player supports common audio formats including MP3, WAV, M4A, and other formats compatible with iOS AVAudioPlayer.

Where are my audio files stored?

All audio files are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, share, or have access to your audio files. Your files remain private and under your control.

How do I delete a project or track?

To delete a project or track, swipe left on the item in the list and tap the delete button. Your audio files will remain in your device's storage.

Do I need an internet connection?

An internet connection is only required for sign-in. Once signed in, you can use all playback and editing features offline. Your audio files are stored locally on your device.

How do I sign in?

Multitrack Player uses Google Sign-In for authentication. Simply tap the sign-in button and follow the prompts to authenticate with your Google account.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your audio files never leave your device. We only collect minimal data necessary for app functionality and analytics. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
